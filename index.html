Untreated Foot Pain Is A Time Bomb

Foot pain is an issue that can’t be ignored, but not simply because it’s difficult to ignore the pain, the main problem is that it develops into something bigger — and may even require surgery in the future.

We are on our feet all the time, working, exercising, or just walking around our homes. Our feet are under stress EVERY day, with no exceptions.

This is a recipe for disaster.

After all, your feet are composed of 26 bones, 33 joints, and more than 120 muscles, ligaments, tendons, and nerves. Together, they serve as the foundation of your body.

Your entire body will collapse if that foundation is in pain or breaks. Poor posture, which appears as back, hip, and neck pain, is caused by tight or immovable feet.

The Truth About Popular Foot Pain “Solutions”

Surgical treatments — like plantar fascia release — are doctors’ favorite solution for foot pain.

These are often costly, hazardous, and take 6 to 12 months to heal.

Finally, we’re left with custom orthotics, which have two well-known problems:

1. They are expensive and require costly visits to the podiatrist;
